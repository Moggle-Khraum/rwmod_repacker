About the Tool:

RWMod was made in hopes that it can help those authors of RWMod to protect their own creation. Do note, the authors have the right whether to use the RWMod Repacker or not, thats on their decision. And since some mods uses codes from other mods, then its depends on the author/s mood. DO NOTE: This tool does not guarantee 100% protection, the tool is still have flaws, and can be used. The protection process it does is called 'Dummying', a combination of dummy creation, encapsulation, and obfuscation.
